hello! i'm priscilla silva, a front-end developer based in malmö, sweden

about me

I have a background in art history and interaction design. It was when I studied the latter that my interest for technology began and I decided to take road of programming. At the moment I am a student at Foo Coding, where I'm preparing to become a full-stack web developer.

I have experience working with HTML, CSS, Javascript and responsive design. I am also proficient in the Adobe Creative Cloud and Figma.

When I am not coding I like to paint, read, go to museums, listen to music and take long walks with my dog Iroko.
